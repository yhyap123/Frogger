Frogger

Appearance:

1. Frog: Round shape and green colour.

2. Car: Square shape and light cyan colour.

3. Log: Rectangle shape and brown colour.

4. Medal: Round shape and gold colour.

5. River: Area with blue colour.

6. Safe zone: Area with purple colour.

Instruction:

1. You are required to score as many points as possible by moving the frog to get the medals located at the top.

2. Each medal will give 200 points and the position of the frog will be reset after getting the medal.

3. The total move up counts, scores obtained for each round and highest scores accumulated will be displayed at the top status bar.

4. How to move the frog:

Arrow Up key - Move Up

Arrow Down key - Move Down

Arrow Left key - Move Left

Arrow Right key - Move Right

5. IMPORTANT: The game is over when the frog dies. There are two conditions where it causes the frog dies.

5a. Collides with the cars.

5b. Falls into rivers.

6. Additional note: The objects will move faster after collecting all the five medals each time.
